Work

CubeScript is a versatile scripting language grounded in the C-style family of programming languages.

Recognized for its syntax reminiscent of Python, Lua, CSharp, and TypeScript, CubeScript offers a fusion of familiar language elements.

The language is meticulously crafted with an emphasis on simplicity, making it both accessible for learning purposes and user-friendly for developers.
